# Technical Exhaustion as Profit-Taking Signal in Momentum Trades

**Thesis:** In momentum continuation trades, extreme overbought conditions (RSI >70 + Bollinger %B >100) provide reliable signals for near-term exhaustion and optimal profit-taking timing.
**Applies to:** general
**Tags:** momentum, technical-exhaustion, profit-taking, overbought, rsi, bollinger, bull, dte-short
**Confidence:** low
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-12

## Analysis

Initial observation from single XLE trade. The combination of RSI entering overbought territory (>70) AND price pushing above the upper Bollinger band (%B >100) coincided with the intraday peak. Exiting on these signals captured nearly the full move (+16.2% peak, sold at peak).

The exit decision was specifically framed as "technical exhaustion signals" and resulted in selling at the exact intraday high. This suggests the signals may have predictive value for short-term exhaustion in momentum setups.

## Exceptions & Nuances

- May not apply in strong parabolic trends where overbought can stay overbought
- Single-day hold — unknown if signals work for multi-day positions
- Requires confirmation from both RSI and Bollinger, not just one
