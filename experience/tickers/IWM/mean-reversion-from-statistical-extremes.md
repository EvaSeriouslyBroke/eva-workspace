## Analysis

Small-caps (IWM) are highly sensitive to liquidity and macro-regime shifts (Yields/Oil). While they frequently bounce from deep oversold RSI (<30), Hindsight analysis from March 2026 has established a non-negotiable rule.

**The "Regime Pivot" Rule:**
1. **Mean Reversion is valid** as long as IWM stays above its 200-day SMA.
2. **Mean Reversion fails** once the 200-day SMA is broken. Structural damage from rising energy costs ($100 Oil) or high yields (10Y > 4.2%) overrides technical oversold signals.
